Data Sources, Attribution & Required Notices

rosterproof · exclusions data feed

RosterProof aggregates and normalizes publicly published government exclusion lists. The data has been modified from its original form (normalized into one schema, NPI-resolved, history-tracked) and is not official government data; the authoritative record is always the source agency's own publication. No source agency — HHS-OIG or any state — endorses this product. Per-source publication URLs and as-of timestamps ship inside the dataset itself (SOURCE_REGISTRY, FEED_INFO).

Required notice — New York OMIG records

"The OMIG List of Restricted and Excluded Providers exists as notification that a person is excluded from the Medicaid program and for the purpose of preventing Medicaid payments for care, services, or supplies, furnished, ordered, or prescribed by that person. Use of the list for other purposes is beyond the scope of its intended use."

Required caveat — Washington HCA records

"Not every action taken by the Department of Social and Health Services (DSHS) or the Health Care Authority (HCA) involves a finding of wrongdoing." Providers terminated without cause under WAC 182-502-0040 are not included on Washington's list.

Not included: Michigan

MDHHS data is withheld from this product pending written redistribution permission or public-records sourcing (michigan.gov terms restrict redistribution of site data).

Accuracy

Every source agency disclaims the accuracy of its published list. Verify any hit with the source agency before taking adverse action; records flagged NEEDS_HUMAN_REVIEW = TRUE in EXCLUSIONS_SCREENABLE must be human-verified.
