WHY IT MATTERS

Screening only the federal list misses half the risk

States exclude providers for reasons that never propagate to the federal LEIE — and when they do propagate, it happens with months of lag. RosterProof closes that gap: every record carries its source, jurisdiction, exclusion window, and an NPI match with stated confidence, so a compliance analyst can defend every hit without asking anyone.
